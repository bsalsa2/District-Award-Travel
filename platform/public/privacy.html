Privacy Policy

What Information We Collect

When you use District Award Travel, we collect:

  • Contact information: Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Travel preferences: Your home airport, travel goals, preferred cabin class, and trip dates
  • Loyalty program information: The names of programs you hold and the balances you choose to share (e.g., "75,000 Chase UR")
  • Elite status: Any elite statuses you hold with airlines or hotel programs

What we never ask for: Account passwords, credit card numbers, banking credentials, or any sensitive account information. You always retain full control of your accounts.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Research award space and transfer options for your travel goals
  • Generate personalized recommendations
  • Communicate with you about our service
  • Invoice you for services rendered

We do not use your information for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

How We Protect Your Information

We protect your information through:

  • Encryption in transit: All data is encrypted as it travels between your device and our servers (HTTPS)
  • Secure hosting: Your data is hosted on Render's secure infrastructure with industry-standard protections
  • Access control: Access to your data is limited to the District Award Travel operator (currently one person)
  • No backups to third parties: Your data is not shared with cloud backup services or external vendors

Analytics & Cookies

We do not use third-party analytics services, cookies, or tracking pixels. We do not track you across websites. We use only first-party measurement: a random session ID stored in your browser's sessionStorage (cleared when you close the tab) to understand our funnel (views → form starts → submissions). This information is never sold or shared.

Data Retention & Deletion

We keep your information while you are an active or past client. If you would like us to delete your information at any time, contact us at districtawardtravel@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Data." We will delete all records associated with you within 7 business days.

Data Sharing

We never sell, share, or transfer your information to third parties. Your information is used only by District Award Travel to serve you. No data brokers, no marketing partners, no exceptions.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what information we hold about you
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data

Contact districtawardtravel@gmail.com to exercise any of these rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of our service constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

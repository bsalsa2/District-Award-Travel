FAQ

Fair questions, straight answers.

Do you need my passwords?
No. Screenshots and balance totals only. You never share login credentials — you share the numbers you choose to tell us.
Do you book for me?
No. We send you a step-by-step guide; you book directly in your own accounts. You stay in full control throughout.
What if you can't beat cash?
You owe nothing. Zero fee. We'll tell you honestly if your route or dates aren't a fit for award redemptions.
Is this legit?
Student-founded, no upfront cost, you only pay after saving. Every savings number is documented with a timestamped cash benchmark screenshot. Details in our Terms.
How long does it take?
Usually 24–72 hours after you share your details. If your route is genuinely hard, we'll tell you upfront instead of leaving you waiting.

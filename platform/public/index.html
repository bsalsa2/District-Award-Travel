FAQ

Fair questions, straight answers.

Do you need my passwords?
No. Balances and screenshots only — never logins. You keep full control of every account.
Do you book for me?
No — you book in your own accounts with our step-by-step guide. We're your navigator, not your pilot.
What if you can't beat cash?
You owe nothing — and we'll tell you quickly rather than waste your time.
Why is there no upfront fee?
Our incentive is to maximize your savings, not charge you regardless. If we can't deliver real savings, we haven't done our job.
Is this legit?
Student-founded in the DC Metro area. $0 upfront; you pay only after documented savings, each backed by a timestamped cash benchmark. Details in our Terms.
How long does it take?
Usually 24–72 hours. For complex or unusual routes we'll tell you upfront before you wait.

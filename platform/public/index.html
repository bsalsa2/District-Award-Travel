── Fee Structure

We win when you win.

Our fee is always 10% of the savings we generate for you. If we can't beat the cash price, you pay nothing. It's that simple.

10%
of savings generated — our only fee
✅
Savings = Cash Price − Points Redemption Cost
We calculate savings as the gap between what the same trip would cost in cash versus what you actually pay out-of-pocket using your points (taxes, fees, and our advisory fee).
✅
No savings = no charge
If we can't find a redemption that beats the cash price, we tell you that upfront and you owe nothing.
✅
No credit card recommendations
We don't sell credit cards or earn referral commissions. Our only revenue is your advisory fee.
🚫
What We Don't Do
We do not recommend credit cards, earn affiliate commissions, or accept payment from airlines or hotels. Our advice is completely independent. If a points redemption doesn't make sense, we'll tell you to pay cash.

Service Description

District Award Travel ("we," "us," "DAT," or "the Service") is an advisory-only service. We research available award flight redemptions using the loyalty program points and travel goals you share with us, then present options for you to evaluate and book yourself.

What We Do
  • Search available award space across airlines and transfer partners for your travel goals
  • Identify redemptions that beat the lowest publicly available cash price for the same routing
  • Benchmark each option with a timestamped screenshot of the cash equivalent
  • Deliver a step-by-step booking guide so you can complete the booking in your own accounts
  • Answer follow-up questions throughout the booking process

You always book your own travel. We research and advise — we do not book on your behalf, we do not access your accounts, and we do not hold your login credentials at any point. Think of us as a knowledgeable navigator: we plan the route; you fly the plane.

By submitting an intake form or communicating with us about a booking, you agree to these terms.

Fees & Payment

Our fee is always 10% of documented savings. There are no upfront fees, retainer fees, subscription fees, or commissions from any airline, hotel, or card issuer.

How Savings Are Calculated
Lowest public cash price − Taxes & fees you actually paid = Your Documented Savings
Our fee = 10% of Documented Savings  ·  Cash price benchmarked by screenshot at time of research  ·  Points used are treated as having no dollar cost for fee-calculation purposes

"Lowest publicly available cash price" means the lowest price visible to a general traveler on the airline's own website or a major OTA (e.g., Google Flights, Kayak) for the same origin, destination, dates, and cabin class at the time we conduct our research. We document this with a timestamped screenshot.

"Taxes and fees actually paid" means the total out-of-pocket cash you paid at the time of booking the award — typically the carrier-imposed fees and government taxes required to ticket the award. The points redeemed are not counted as a cash cost.

Zero Fee Scenarios
  • We cannot find an award redemption that beats the cash price — you pay $0
  • You review our options and choose not to book any of them — you pay $0
  • Award space disappears before you can book — you pay $0

Fees are invoiced after your booking is confirmed and you provide us with confirmation details so we can calculate the exact savings. Payment is due within 14 calendar days of the invoice date. We accept payment via the methods listed in the invoice.

If the documented savings calculation is disputed, contact us at districtawardtravel@gmail.com within 14 days of the invoice and we will review the benchmark together.

Award Availability

Award space on airlines and hotel programs changes constantly — sometimes within minutes. Seats and rooms are controlled entirely by the carrier or property, and availability can open or disappear at any time without notice.

Important

We cannot guarantee that an award we identify at research time will still be available when you attempt to book. We present options that are available at the time of our research. It is possible — and not uncommon — for availability to change between when we send you the options and when you click "Book."

When a specific option disappears before booking, we will make reasonable efforts to find an alternative redemption at no additional research charge. However, if no comparable alternative exists, no fee is owed because no savings have been documented.

We also cannot control or predict program devaluations, transfer partner changes, routing rule adjustments, or award chart modifications. We research based on the rules in effect at the time of our work.

No Refunds

Because District Award Travel is an advisory service, our work product is delivered when we send you research results and a booking guide. Once options have been delivered to you, no refunds are issued — the service has been rendered.

We structure our fee model to avoid situations where you pay before receiving value: you owe nothing until you have booked a confirmed trip using our options. If you receive options and choose not to book any of them, you owe nothing. There is nothing to refund because no fee was charged.

If you believe you were invoiced incorrectly — for example, if the cash price benchmark was inaccurate — contact us within 14 days of the invoice and we will review and correct any error in good faith.

1. What We Do

District Award Travel researches award travel redemptions using points and miles programs. We identify award space, calculate potential savings against comparable cash fares, and deliver our findings to you as a written report with step-by-step booking instructions.

We do not book travel on your behalf. We do not handle your login credentials. We do not access any of your travel or loyalty accounts directly.

2. What We Don't Do

To be explicit about scope:

No passwords. We will never ask for your airline or hotel account passwords. If someone claiming to be us asks for a password, that is not us.

No credit card handling. We do not collect, store, or transmit credit card numbers. Our fee is invoiced separately after research is delivered.

No booking on your behalf. You book in your own accounts using the instructions we provide. You remain in full control of your accounts at all times.

3. Payment

Our fee is 10% of your documented savings versus the lowest comparable cash price for the same itinerary at the time of research. If we can't beat the cash price, you owe us nothing — zero fee, no exceptions.

We will invoice you after we send your award options and you confirm you have successfully booked the trip. Payment is due within 14 days of invoice. We document our cash benchmark with a timestamped screenshot so the savings calculation is transparent and verifiable.

We reserve the right to adjust pricing or fee structure for future engagements with advance notice.

4. Accuracy & Availability

Award space is a live, constantly-changing inventory controlled by airlines and hotels. Options we present reflect availability at the time we complete our research. We cannot guarantee that the same award space will still be available by the time you attempt to book.

We recommend booking as quickly as possible after receiving your options. We are not liable for award space that disappears between the time of our research and the time of your booking attempt.

5. Data

We store the information you provide to us: your name, email address, travel preferences, travel dates, and loyalty program point balances. We do not store credit card numbers, bank account information, or account passwords.

See our Privacy Policy for full details on how your data is stored and protected.

6. No Warranty

District Award Travel is a student-run service. We put genuine effort into every research request and stand behind our savings calculations. However, we make no guarantees about specific outcomes, award availability, or the continued existence of any loyalty program or award chart.

The service is provided "as is." To the extent permitted by law, we disclaim all warranties, express or implied, including any warranty of fitness for a particular purpose.

Terms of Service

1. What We Do

District Award Travel ("we," "us," or "the Service") provides advisory services to help you find valuable award travel redemptions using your loyalty program points. We:

  • Research available award space and transfer partners for your travel goals
  • Generate recommendations for you to evaluate
  • Provide guidance on booking your chosen award

You always book your own travel. We never access your accounts, never hold your passwords, and never execute bookings on your behalf.

2. What You Do

When you use the Service, you:

  • Provide accurate information about your travel goals, dates, and loyalty program balances
  • Review the recommendations we provide
  • Make your own decision about which option (if any) to book
  • Execute the booking yourself in your own accounts
  • Confirm your actual savings before paying our fee

3. Our Fee

Our fee is always 10% of the documented savings we generate for you.

Savings = Cash price (timestamped benchmark) − Everything you pay out of pocket (award taxes + fees).

If we cannot beat the cash price or if you choose not to book, you pay zero. There are no upfront fees, booking fees, or hidden costs.

Our invoice is issued after your booking is confirmed and your savings are documented. Payment is due within 14 days of invoice.

4. Award Availability

Award space on airlines and hotels changes constantly. Availability we identify may disappear before you book. We cannot guarantee that a specific award will be available at booking time. If your preferred option sells out, we will help you identify alternatives.

5. Affiliations & Disclosures

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or acting on behalf of any airline, hotel, bank, credit card issuer, or loyalty program. We do not receive commissions from any program. Our only source of revenue is the fee we earn from documented client savings.

6. Limitation of Liability

Our recommendations are provided in good faith based on available data. We are not responsible for:

  • Program devaluations, award chart changes, or airline/hotel policy changes after we provide our recommendations
  • Errors in your booking (you execute bookings yourself)
  • Denied or cancelled bookings due to your account status or program rules
  • Changes in award availability or pricing

Our total liability is capped at the fees you have paid us. We provide no guarantees of specific savings amounts or award availability.
